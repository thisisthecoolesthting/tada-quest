# Tada Quest Privacy Policy Draft

Effective date: [DATE]

Tada Quest is a kids activity app that teaches safe home magic missions. The app is designed to work without accounts, advertising, analytics, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or social features.

## Information we collect

Tada Quest does not collect personal information from children or adults. The app does not ask for names, email addresses, birthdays, photos, voice recordings, location, contacts, or messages.

## Local progress

The app may save mission progress and badges on the device only. This information stays on the device and is not sent to us.

## Third parties

The Android app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social SDKs, or sign-in SDKs.

## Website

Our website provides app information, safety notes, and this privacy policy. We do not intentionally use cookies or tracking pixels. Hosting providers may process basic server logs for security and operation.

## Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will delete it.
