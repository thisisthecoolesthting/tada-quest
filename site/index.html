Clean up after

Every mission avoids fire, sharp tools, glass, chemicals, and medicine. Missions that use water, coins, clips, soap, or small objects are clearly labeled “Grown-up helps” and include a safety check and cleanup reminder.

For Parents

Tada Quest is offline-first and privacy-friendly. Kids don't create accounts or share personal information. The app has no ads, no analytics, no camera, no microphone, and no location — and requests no internet permission at all.

Each mission teaches what the audience sees, the items needed, a step-by-step setup, a kid-friendly secret, a short “showtime” script, and safety and cleanup reminders. It's about confidence, creativity, science, and performance — not scary magic or unsafe stunts.
