Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

Tada Quest is a kids activity app that teaches safe home magic missions. The app is designed to work without accounts, advertising, analytics, camera, microphone, location, contacts, or social features.

Information we collect

Tada Quest does not collect personal information from children or adults. The app does not ask for names, email addresses, birthdays, photos, voice recordings, location, contacts, or messages. The Android app requests no internet permission.

Local progress

The app may save mission progress and badges on the device only. This information stays on the device and is not sent to us or anyone else.

Third parties

The Android app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, social SDKs, or sign-in SDKs.

Website

This website provides app information, safety notes, and this privacy policy. We do not intentionally use cookies or tracking pixels. Our hosting provider may process basic server logs (such as IP addresses) for security and operation, as is standard for any website.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us and we will delete it. We aim to comply with COPPA and the Google Play Families policy.
